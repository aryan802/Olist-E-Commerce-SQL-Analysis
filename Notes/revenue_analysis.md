# Revenue Analysis

## Objective

The purpose of this analysis is to understand revenue generation patterns across the Olist marketplace. The analysis focuses on overall revenue performance, growth trends, geographic contribution, product category performance, seller contribution, payment methods, and the relationship between revenue and customer satisfaction.

---

# 1. Revenue Overview

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------:|
| Product Revenue | 13,591,643.70 |
| Freight Value | 2,251,909.54 |
| Customer Spend | 16,008,872.12 |
| Average Order Value | 137.75 |

### Observations

- The marketplace generated over **13.59 million** in product sales.
- Customers spent approximately **16.01 million** overall.
- Freight charges contributed significantly to total customer spending.
- The average order value was **137.75**.

### Insight

Customer spend exceeds product revenue by approximately **2.42 million**, with freight accounting for the majority of this difference.

# Key Revenue Findings

1. The marketplace generated **13.59 million** in product revenue and **16.01 million** in customer spend.
2. Revenue increased significantly from 2017 to 2018, demonstrating strong growth.
3. November 2017 was the highest revenue month, indicating seasonal demand effects.
4. São Paulo is the dominant revenue-generating state, contributing approximately 38% of total revenue.
5. Health & Beauty, Watches & Gifts, and Home & Bath are the marketplace's strongest product categories.
6. Revenue is distributed across many sellers, reducing dependence on any single seller.
7. Credit cards dominate transaction value and represent the primary payment method.
8. Several high-revenue categories exhibit below-average review scores, presenting opportunities for business improvement.

---

# Conclusion

The Olist marketplace experienced strong growth throughout the observed period, supported by a diverse seller base, high customer spending, and strong category performance. Revenue is concentrated geographically in southeastern Brazil and product-wise in a small group of leading categories. Future analysis should focus on customer retention, repeat purchasing behavior, and seller performance to identify opportunities for sustainable growth.
